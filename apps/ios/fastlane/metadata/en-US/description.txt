MAZE is a therapist-controlled parenting skills toolkit that helps parents practice evidence-based techniques with their children.

FOR THERAPISTS
• Manage your client list
• Unlock skills session-by-session based on progress
• Track practice logs and engagement
• Structured notes with privacy-first design

FOR PARENTS  
• Access unlocked skill cheat sheets
• "In the Moment" coaching for real-time guidance
• Practice logging with emoji ratings
• Progressive skill unlocking based on therapist recommendations

HOW IT WORKS
1. Therapist creates an account and adds clients
2. Parents receive an invite code and connect
3. Therapist unlocks skills as parents demonstrate mastery
4. Parents practice techniques and log progress
5. Therapist reviews logs and adjusts the skill tree

EVIDENCE-BASED
MAZE is built on PCIT (Parent-Child Interaction Therapy) and BPT (Behavioral Parent Training) curricula – proven approaches used by clinicians worldwide.

PRIVACY
MAZE is designed with clinical privacy in mind. No personal health information (PHI) is stored in v1. All data is encrypted and protected by row-level security.
