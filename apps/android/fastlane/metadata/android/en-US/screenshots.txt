PRODUCTION NOTES FOR ALL FIVE:
  - Realistic-looking but never identifying — use names like "Jordan" (parent), "Sam" (child reference), "Dr. M." (therapist placeholder). Never a real name.
  - The "In the Moment" screen MUST show the 988/911/therapist escalation bar — this is both a product feature and a defensible safety claim
  - Do NOT include any image of a real or cartoon child — Play Store policy + clinical ethics
  - Status bar: 9:41 AM, full battery, full signal — universally recognized as a "polished app" cue
  - PNG, sRGB, device-native portrait orientation
  - Recommended: 1080 x 1920 px minimum
  - Match the ParentScript brand exactly (check brand.html for the color tokens — do not invent colors)
  - Typography: large, high-contrast, scannable. The parent opening this app in panic mode should be able to read every word.
