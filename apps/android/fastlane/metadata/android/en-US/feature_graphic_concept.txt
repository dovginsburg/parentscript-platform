  - Sub-headline ("MAZE brings you back..."): ~28pt regular, mid-gray (#6B7280)

COLOR PALETTE (suggested — match the existing brand):

  - Primary text: #1F2937

  - Brand teal: #2D7D8F (or whatever the ParentScript primary is — check brand.html)

DO NOT INCLUDE:
  - Photos of real children (Play Store policy + ethical issues for a clinical app for minors)
  - Cartoon/illustrated children (same reason — better to keep it abstract)
  - Crisis hotline numbers (those live in the app, not the marketing graphic)
  - Pricing text
  - Too many elements — the eye should land on the phone and the headline, period
  - Generic stock photos of "happy families"

REASONING: For a clinical app for parents in hard moments, the marketing graphic needs to feel calm, competent, and not condescending. The phone showing the actual signature UI element does the heavy lifting; the headline delivers the emotional promise. Avoid imagery that makes the parent's struggle feel performative.
