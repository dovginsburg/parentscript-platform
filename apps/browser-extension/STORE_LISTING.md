### Full Description (16,000 chars max)

ParentScript is a therapist-controlled parenting skills companion. This extension gives you instant access to your unlocked skill cheat sheets from any webpage.

**How It Works:**

1. Your child's therapist unlocks parenting skills for you session-by-session
2. Right-click any text → "Search ParentScript for..." to find relevant skills
3. Press Cmd+Shift+P (Mac) or Ctrl+Shift+P (Windows) to open the skill search
4. Click any skill to view the full cheat sheet on parentscript.app

**Features:**

• **Quick Search** — Find skills by keyword from any webpage
• **Context Menu** — Right-click selected text to search directly
• **Keyboard Shortcut** — Cmd+Shift+P / Ctrl+Shift+P opens search
• **Direct Links** — One click to open full skill cheat sheets
• **Privacy-First** — No tracking, no ads, no data collection
• **Offline Support** — Skills cached for offline access (coming soon)

**What ParentScript Is:**

ParentScript is parenting support — not therapy. The tools, scripts, and resources are educational and self-coaching aids built around well-established parenting frameworks (PCIT, BPT, CPS, Triple P, Circle of Security, DBT-informed distress tolerance). They are not a substitute for diagnosis, psychotherapy, crisis care, or treatment of any mental health condition.

If you are working with a licensed mental health professional, ParentScript is designed to support that work between sessions — not replace it.

**Crisis Resources:**

ParentScript is not a crisis service. If you or your child are in crisis:
• Call 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline)
• Text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line)
• Call 911
• Call the Childhelp National Child Abuse Hotline: 1-800-422-4453

**Requirements:**

• A ParentScript account (free, provided by your child's therapist)
• Skills unlocked by your therapist
• Chrome 120+ or Edge 120+

**Privacy:**

This extension:
• Does NOT collect any personal data
• Does NOT track your browsing
• Does NOT sell your information
• Only connects to ParentScript's secure API when you actively search

See our full privacy policy: https://parentscript.app/privacy

**Support:**

Questions? Email support@parentscript.app

**About ParentScript:**

ParentScript is built by AMAZED Labs (Dov + Ariella), a clinical-tech studio focused on evidence-based mental health tools. Clinical content is reviewed by licensed child psychologists.
