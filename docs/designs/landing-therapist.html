how it works

a clinical tool, not a content app.

every feature in ParentScript was built to extend your work in session — not replace it. you stay in control. families stay supported between sessions.

you control what each family sees

unlock skills session by session. lock skills when a family needs to revisit. parents only see what you've approved.

structured session notes

capture what you tried, what worked, and what to revisit next time. notes attach to the family, not the device.

"in-the-moment" coaching

parents open the app during the meltdown — not after. one tap to a script, a breathing prompt, or a hot-line. you set which.

practice logging between sessions

parents log what they tried. you see a real picture of adherence — not just what they remember in the next session.

evidence-based cheat sheets

every script cites its framework. PCIT, BPT, CPS, Triple P — Mira's clinical team signs off on every word that ships.

safety rail comes first

if a parent enters content flagged as a crisis class, 988 and 911 surface immediately. no LLM is allowed to "respond."

clinical authority

every word, signed off by a clinician.

dr. ariella eisenberg, psyd — clinical advisor to ParentScript — reviews every skill cheat sheet before it ships. frameworks are cited on every script, never paraphrased away.
